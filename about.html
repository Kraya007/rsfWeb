About Us

Top-Rated Tutoring Excellence in Soweto

RSF Tutorials is an independent organization established by a Group of like-minded youths with experience in different sectors to promote youth capacity development, Offer educational services (tutoring) and provide awareness to promote social skills, life skills and also provide opportunities to youth. RSF Tutorials is an academic reinforcement intervention programme for grade 1 to 12. The programme pride's itself in working with schools in developing a world class learner who will serve the nation with excellence.

About Us

RSF is a Christian-based academy, and our name stands for "The Righteous Shall Live by Faith." This means that those who follow God's teachings and trust in His promises will live a life of faith and receive spiritual growth and eternal salvation. While we welcome and respect people of all religions, RSF is proudly Black-owned and recognized as a Level 1 contributor under the B-BBEE system.
