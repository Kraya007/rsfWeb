Phases We Offer

At RSF, we provide academic intervention for learners from Grade 1 to Grade 12. This helps learners stay in the same system throughout their school years, creating consistency and strong connections with tutors. We adjust lessons to fit each learner's learning style and pace, offering personalized help whether they are starting school or preparing for exams. Our focus on exam preparation, like for matric exams, helps learners succeed. We also track each learner's progress to ensure they keep growing academically and personally. We follow CAPS for all phases, from science to humanities, and the fee is R250.00 for each phase.

Foundation Phase

We proudly assist Grade 1 to 3 learners at Tholimfundo, building a strong foundation for their future success.

Intermediate Phase

At our Tholimfundo branch, we assist Intermediate Phase learners (Grades 4 to 6) with the tools and guidance they need to thrive and excel on their academic journey!

Senior Phase

At our Faranani branch, we engage and inspire Senior Phase students (Grades 7 to 9) with dynamic learning experiences. We focus on building critical skills, boosting confidence, and preparing them for the academic challenges ahead.

Further Education and Training phase

At our Faranani branch, we offer specialized support for FET students (Grades 10 to 12), focusing on mastering complex subjects, achieving top exam results, and preparing for university and career opportunities. Our tailored approach includes personalized tutoring, exam strategies, and career guidance to ensure students thrive and reach their full potential.

Our Branches

At RSF in Protea Glen, Soweto, we have two branches: Tholimfundo for younger kids and Faranani for older students. This setup helps us teach kids in ways that suit their ages. Tholimfundo covers basics, while Faranani offers advanced subjects. It also helps kids grow socially and emotionally by being with peers their own age and allows us to provide better resources and support for each group.

About Us

RSF Tutorials is an independent, non-government and non-profitable social organization established by a Group of like-minded youths with experience in different sectors to promote youth capacity development, Offer educational services (tutoring) and provide awareness to promote social skills, life skills and also provide opportunities to youth. RSF Tutorials is an academic reinforcement intervention programme for grade 1 to 12. The programme pride's itself in working with schools in developing a world class learner who will serve the nation with excellence.
